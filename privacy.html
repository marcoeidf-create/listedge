1. Information We Collect

When you interact with ListEdge — whether through our website, checkout, or a strategy call — we may collect:

  • Contact details: name, email address, company name, and any information you submit through forms or during onboarding.
  • Order information: details related to list purchases, including plan selected and delivery preferences.
  • Usage data: pages visited, time on site, and technical information such as browser type and device — collected anonymously to improve the experience.
  • Cookies: small files stored on your device that help us remember preferences and understand how you navigate the site.

2. How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Fulfill and deliver your list orders.
  • Respond to inquiries and schedule strategy calls.
  • Send order updates and relevant communications — only with your consent.
  • Improve our website and service based on aggregate usage patterns.

We don't use your data for advertising, and we don't profile you beyond what's needed to serve you well.

3. Storage & Security

Your information is stored on secure servers with restricted access. We apply reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

No system is completely immune to risk, but protecting your data is something we take seriously — not just a checkbox.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Full stop.

We may share limited data with trusted third-party tools — such as payment processors, CRM platforms, or email delivery services — solely to operate our business. Every partner we work with is held to the same privacy standards we apply internally.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of ListEdge or its customers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Request a summary of what data we hold about you.

To exercise any of these rights, reach us at support@listedge.co. We'll respond promptly.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to understand how visitors use our site and to improve performance. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling cookies may affect some functionality.

We don't use cookies to serve ads or track you across third-party sites.

7. External Links

Our site may link to third-party websites. ListEdge is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites. We recommend reviewing their policies directly.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date. We'll notify you of material changes where appropriate.

9. Contact

Questions about how we handle your data? We're straightforward about it.
