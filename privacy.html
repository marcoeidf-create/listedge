Privacy Policy

ListEdge collects only what is operationally necessary, retains data securely, and does not sell or trade personal information under any circumstances.

Last updated: October 2025 GDPR Compliant
1. Information we collect 2. How we use it 3. Storage & security 4. Sharing your data 5. Your rights 6. Cookies 7. External links 8. Policy updates 9. Contact

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with ListEdge — whether through our website, checkout, or a strategy call — we may collect the following categories of data:

  • Contact information: name, business email address, company name, and any information submitted through forms or during onboarding.
  • Transaction data: details related to list purchases, including plan selected and delivery preferences.
  • Usage data: pages visited, session duration, and technical information such as browser type and device — collected anonymously to improve the service.
  • Cookie data: small files stored on your device that help us retain preferences and understand site navigation patterns.

2. How We Use It

We use collected information solely for operational and service delivery purposes:

  • To fulfill and deliver your contact list orders.
  • To respond to inquiries and coordinate strategy calls.
  • To send order confirmations and service communications — only with your consent.
  • To improve our website and service based on aggregate usage patterns.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not conduct behavioral profiling beyond what is necessary to serve you effectively.

3. Storage & Security

Your information is stored on secure servers with restricted access controls. We apply industry-standard technical and administrative safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

No system is entirely immune to risk. However, data security is an operational priority for ListEdge — not a compliance checkbox.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

Limited data may be shared with trusted service providers — such as payment processors, CRM platforms, or email delivery infrastructure — strictly to operate our business. All third-party partners are contractually required to meet the same data protection standards we apply internally.

Disclosure may also occur when required by applicable law or to protect the legal rights and safety of ListEdge or its clients.

5. Your Rights

You are entitled to the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access, correction, or deletion of personal information we hold about you.
  • Withdrawal of consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • A summary of what data we retain on your behalf.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@listedge.co. We will respond within a commercially reasonable timeframe.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to understand how visitors navigate our site and to improve performance. Cookies can be managed or disabled through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit certain site functionality.

We do not deploy cookies for advertising purposes or to track users across third-party sites.

7. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. ListEdge is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We recommend reviewing their respective policies directly.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy periodically. Any material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Where appropriate, we will notify affected parties of significant amendments.

9. Contact

For questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us through the following channels:

Email: support@listedge.co
Website: www.listedge.co
